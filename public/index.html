About PlagCheck

Advanced plagiarism detection for text and code

How It Works

PlagCheck uses advanced algorithms to search the web for content similar to your text or code. We analyze the results and provide a similarity score to help you identify potential plagiarism.

Features

  • Check text and code for plagiarism
  • Highlight matched content for easy identification
  • View source URLs for matched content
  • Download detailed reports
  • Check history for reviewing past results

Tips for Using PlagCheck

  • For best results, check content in chunks of 500-1000 words
  • Use the appropriate content type (text, code, HTML)
  • Review each matched source carefully
  • Remember that some similar phrasing may be coincidental

Privacy & Security

We take your privacy seriously. Your content is only used for the purpose of checking plagiarism and is not stored permanently on our servers.

Limitations

While our tool is highly effective, no plagiarism checker is 100% accurate. Some content may not be accessible to our search algorithms, and some similarities may be coincidental.
